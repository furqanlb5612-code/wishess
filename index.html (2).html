💌 Dil Ki Baat

Yaar, tum meri zindagi mein aaye aur sab kuch badal gaya.
Tumhari dosti mere liye uss roshni ki tarah hai,
jo andhere mein bhi raasta dikhati hai.

Har mushkil mein tumne haath thama,
har khushi mein sath hansay — yeh rishta kuch alag hi hai.
Tum sirf ek dost nahi, tum mera woh yaar ho
jis par main aankhein band kar ke bharosa karta hun.

Is khaas din par main chahta hun ke tum jaano —
meri duaain, meri mohabbat, meri khushiyan
sab tumhare saath hain. ❤️

Allah tumhe woh sab kuch de jo tumhare dil mein hai.
Khushiyan ho, sehat ho, kamiyabi ho —
aur hazaaron saal meri dosti mile tumhe! 🥂

— Tumhara Dost 💛
